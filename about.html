About NinjaNumberTees

This is the official website of NinjaNumberTees.

NinjaNumberTees is an independent creative apparel brand for people who live inside spreadsheets, dashboards, code editors, query windows and performance reports.

Operated from the Netherlands with an international audience in mind.

Mission

Turn the daily language of data, analytics and technical work into sharp, wearable designs that feel specific to the people doing the work.

Vision

Build a recognizable global niche brand for spreadsheet users, analysts, BI teams, developers and technical operators who want humor with precision.

Design Philosophy

The visual system is dark, tactical and direct: high contrast artwork, readable text, practical product metadata and no generic corporate filler.

Audience

Data analysts, Excel power users, dashboard builders, software developers, automation operators, AI practitioners and teams who turn chaos into structured output.

Quality

Design entries are structured with titles, categories, descriptions, tags and asset references so product publishing can stay consistent across platforms.

Creativity and Humor

The tone is witty, technical and concise. NinjaNumberTees avoids vague slogans and focuses on recognizable work moments.
