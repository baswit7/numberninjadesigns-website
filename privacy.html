Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

Country of Operation: Netherlands

NinjaNumberTees respects privacy and operates this public website with limited data collection, transparent contact handling and official platform API usage only.

Contact Information

Privacy requests can be sent to privacy@ninjanumbertees.com. This address is used for privacy and data protection questions.

GDPR Statement

For individuals in the European Economic Area, NinjaNumberTees supports applicable GDPR rights, including access, correction, deletion, restriction and objection where legally available.

Information Collection

The website does not offer account registration. Information may be collected only when a visitor sends an email or when official platform APIs provide data for authorized NinjaNumberTees brand accounts.

Use of Information

Information is used to respond to inquiries, process privacy requests, support API reviews, manage authorized brand content workflows and maintain accurate product or listing operations.

Third Party Services

NinjaNumberTees may use official services from Pinterest, Meta, Instagram, TikTok, Google, Reddit, Printify and Etsy. Those services operate under their own privacy policies and account controls.

Data Retention

Contact emails are retained only as long as reasonably needed for the request, record keeping or legal compliance. Platform API data is retained only for authorized brand operations and approved use cases.

Data Security

NinjaNumberTees applies practical access limitation, least-privilege workflows and avoids collecting credentials or passwords. No public website feature asks visitors for platform login details.

Children's Privacy

This website and related internal brand tools are not directed to children and do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

International Transfers

Because external platforms may operate internationally, data handled through official APIs may be processed according to those providers' regional infrastructure and policies.

User Rights

Users may request access, correction or deletion by email. External platform account data should also be managed through the relevant platform's own privacy tools.

Contact for Privacy Requests

Email privacy@ninjanumbertees.com with a clear description of the request and the platform or communication involved.
