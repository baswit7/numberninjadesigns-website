Developer / API Use Case

Official Brand: NumberNinjaTees

Official Website: https://www.ninjanumbertees.com

Organization: Independent creative apparel brand

Country: Netherlands

Developer Contact: api@ninjanumbertees.com

Purpose: NumberNinjaTees Studio is used internally to manage product content, creative assets, listings, metadata, publishing preparation and performance insights for the NumberNinjaTees brand.

Official API Usage

API access is requested only for authorized NumberNinjaTees brand accounts and only through official platform APIs, approved permissions and OAuth flows where required.

Pinterest

Purpose: Prepare, publish and analyze brand content. Official API: Pinterest API. OAuth: Used for authorized brand accounts. Human Approval: Required for publishing-sensitive workflows. Allowed Usage: Brand pins, boards, creative metadata and analytics. Data Usage: Account-authorized content and performance data. Restrictions: No scraping, no unauthorized posting. Compliance: Only approved scopes. Privacy: See privacy policy. Security: Least privilege.

Meta

Purpose: Manage brand content preparation and insights. Official API: Meta platform APIs. OAuth: Used when required. Human Approval: Required before public posting. Allowed Usage: Authorized brand account workflows. Data Usage: Brand content and permitted analytics. Restrictions: No credential collection. Compliance: Platform policy aligned. Privacy: Limited use. Security: Access controlled.

Instagram

Purpose: Prepare and review brand media workflows. Official API: Instagram platform APIs. OAuth: Used for authorized accounts. Human Approval: Required for publishing. Allowed Usage: Brand media, captions and insights. Data Usage: Approved account data. Restrictions: No login automation. Compliance: Official permissions only. Privacy: No resale. Security: Least privilege.

TikTok

Purpose: Support brand content preparation and performance review. Official API: TikTok APIs. OAuth: Official OAuth only. Human Approval: Required for publishing-sensitive actions. Allowed Usage: Authorized brand content workflows. Data Usage: Approved content and metrics. Restrictions: No scraping or password storage. Compliance: Platform policy aligned. Privacy: Minimal retention. Security: Token access limited.

Google

Purpose: Support authorized brand operations, business metadata and review workflows. Official API: Google APIs. OAuth: Used where required. Human Approval: Used for sensitive changes. Allowed Usage: Approved brand workflows. Data Usage: Data required for the approved use case. Restrictions: No unauthorized data access. Compliance: Approved scopes only. Privacy: Request-based handling. Security: Scoped access.

Reddit

Purpose: Support compliant brand account and community-related workflows. Official API: Reddit API. OAuth: Official authorization only. Human Approval: Required for posting. Allowed Usage: Brand-related content workflows. Data Usage: Approved account data. Restrictions: No surveillance or scraping. Compliance: Reddit policy aligned. Privacy: Limited use. Security: Least privilege.

Printify

Purpose: Product preparation, fulfillment preparation and catalog operations. Official API: Printify API. OAuth: Used if required by platform flow. Human Approval: Required for product publishing decisions. Allowed Usage: Product and fulfillment metadata. Data Usage: Product operations only. Restrictions: No unrelated account access. Compliance: Approved platform usage. Privacy: Minimal operational data. Security: Restricted access.

Etsy

Purpose: Listing management, product metadata and shop operations. Official API: Etsy API. OAuth: Used for authorized shop access. Human Approval: Required for listing and shop-sensitive changes. Allowed Usage: Authorized shop workflows. Data Usage: Listing, product and shop metadata. Restrictions: No credential collection or scraping. Compliance: Etsy policy aligned. Privacy: Shop operations only. Security: Scoped access.

Security Principles

  • Least Privilege
  • Official OAuth
  • No Credential Collection
  • No Password Storage
  • No Login Automation
  • No Scraping
  • No Reverse Engineering
  • Human Approval
  • Respect Platform Policies

Data Handling

Data is used only for authorized brand operations, product metadata, content preparation, publishing review and performance analysis within approved permissions.

Data Retention

Operational API data is retained only as long as needed for approved workflows, troubleshooting, compliance review or platform requirements.

Incident Reporting

Suspected platform, privacy or security incidents can be reported through the official contact address and will be reviewed with priority.
