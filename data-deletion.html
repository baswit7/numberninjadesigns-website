Data Deletion

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

NumberNinjaDesigns accepts deletion requests for contact records and data handled through authorized brand workflows.

How to Request Deletion

Email the NumberNinjaDesigns privacy team with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and describe the data, platform or communication involved.

Processing

Requests are reviewed based on the information available to NumberNinjaDesigns. If the relevant data cannot be identified, NumberNinjaDesigns may request clarification.

External Platforms

Pinterest, Meta, Instagram, TikTok, Reddit, Google, Etsy and Printify manage their own user data, account controls and deletion workflows.

Privacy Contact

Deletion and privacy questions use the same official contact address: the NumberNinjaDesigns privacy team.
